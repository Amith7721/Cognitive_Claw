# SKILL: Meeting Preparation
## Trigger: Calendar event detected within 30 minutes
## Input: Meeting title, attendee list, time

### Behavior:
1. Fetch the meeting title and attendee names from Google Calendar.
2. Compose a concise preparation brief including:
   - Meeting purpose (inferred from title)
   - Key focus areas
   - Suggested talking points
3. Deliver the brief via the in-app Meeting Briefs screen.

### Output Format:
- Heading: Meeting title
- Body: 3-5 bullet points of preparation tips
- Tone: Professional, concise, actionable
